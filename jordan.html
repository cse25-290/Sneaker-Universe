History of Jordan Brand

Jordan Brand was created by Nike in 1984 for basketball legend Micheal Jordan. The first Air Jordan sneaker was released in 1985 and quickly became one of the most influential sneakers in history. Today Jordan sneakers are worn around the world for basketball, fashion and sneaker culture.
